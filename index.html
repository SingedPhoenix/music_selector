Welcome to the Music by Decade Generator

Nothing can define a decade more than the music derived from those eras. Each decade has its own style, class, and way of expressing itself, as did the music from each decade.

What if we can take the best music from each decade, aggregate them into a single playlist, and then be able to offer them as we demanded them?
